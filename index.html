About

Dmitry Bivol is a professional boxer from Russia who competes in the light heavyweight division. He was born on December 18, 1990, in Kyrgyzstan and began his professional boxing career in 2014. Bivol is known for his impressive record of 18 wins with 11 of them being by knockout. He is a former WBA light heavyweight champion, having held the title from 2017 to 2021. Bivol is known for his technical skill and powerful punching, which has made him a formidable opponent in the ring. He is widely regarded as one of the top light heavyweights in the world and is expected to continue to have a successful career in boxing.

Start

Bivol's boxing career began in 2014, and he quickly made a name for himself in the sport. He has won 18 fights, 11 of which were by knockout. Bivol's impressive record and skills in the ring earned him the WBA light heavyweight title, which he held from 2017 to 2021. His technical ability and powerful punches make him a dangerous opponent in the ring.

Achievements

Dmitry Bivol won the WBA light heavyweight title in 2017, defeating Trent Broadhurst by knockout. He successfully defended the title six times before relinquishing it in 2021.

Bivol is a former amateur boxing champion, having won gold medals in the Russian National Championships and the World Combat Games in 2013.

In addition to his boxing accomplishments, Bivol graduated with honors from the Russian State University of Physical Education, Sport, Youth and Tourism.
